Privacy Policy for Workout Timer Last updated: December 26, 2025 1. Introduction Welcome to Workout Timer. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application. 2. Data Stored Locally Your workout data (Workouts, Sessions, and History) is stored locally on your device using an Isar database. We do not have access to this data, and it is not uploaded to our servers. 3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob) We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. To provide these services, Google may collect certain information from your device, including: Advertising IDs (such as Apple's IDFA or Android's Advertising ID). Device information (model, operating system, IP address). Interaction data (which ads you saw or clicked). This data is used to provide personalized ads (with your consent) and to analyze ad performance. 4. Consent Management (GDPR & CCPA) For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we display a consent form (UMP SDK) at the first launch of the app. You can choose to: Accept: To see personalized ads based on your interests. Manage Options: To customize your choices or reject certain data processing. You can change your mind at any time by going to the Settings page within the app and clicking on "Privacy Settings". 5. Data Retention Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or manually clear the history. Advertising data is managed by Google according to their own retention policies. 6.
