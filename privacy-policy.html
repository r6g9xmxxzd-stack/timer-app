Privacy Policy for Workout Timer

Last updated: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Workout Timer. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Stored Locally

Your workout data (Workouts, Sessions, and History) is stored locally on your device using an Isar database. We do not have access to this data, and it is not uploaded to our servers.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. To provide these services, Google may collect certain information from your device, including: Advertising IDs (such as Apple's IDFA or Android's Advertising ID), Device information (model, operating system, IP address), and Interaction data.

4. Consent Management (GDPR & CCPA)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we display a consent form (UMP SDK) at the first launch of the app. You can change your mind at any time by going to the Settings page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: SupportDevApp@proton.me
